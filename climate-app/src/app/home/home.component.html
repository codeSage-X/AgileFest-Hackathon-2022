What is Afrosphere?

Afrosphere was build to serve as a one stop shop for climate change related information.
It serves to inform the you of the effects of climate change in africa as well as motivate you to contribute positively to the environment
This app possess feature which serve to contribute positively to the problem of climate change in Africa.
